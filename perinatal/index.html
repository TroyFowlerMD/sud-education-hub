Eat-Sleep-Console (ESC) Assessment

Function-based NOWS care approach. Replaces numeric Finnegan scoring with three functional assessments (eat, sleep, console) to guide treatment decisions. (Grossman et al., Pediatrics 2017; Yale New Haven ESC model)

Recommended Nonpharmacologic Interventions:

  • Skin-to-skin contact (kangaroo care)
  • Breastfeeding support & encouragement
  • Rooming-in with mother ("Mom is medicine")
  • Swaddling with gentle containment
  • Low-stimulation environment (dim lights, low noise)
  • Small frequent feeds; pacifier for non-nutritive sucking
  • Gentle rocking/soothing; minimize handling
Escalation Pathway: If any ESC domain fails despite optimized nonpharmacologic care → Team huddle → Optimize environment → If still failing after 30-60 min → Consider PRN morphine 0.05-0.1 mg/kg PO q3-4h → If requiring ≥3 PRN doses in 24h → Consider scheduled morphine with wean protocol
